What can you do with view.xml

Assume you have a view.xml in theme folder (app/design/frontend/<Vendor>/<Theme>/etc), an alternative  you can get a view.xml  default from the blank theme in vendor folder.

So, what can you do with it?

  1. Change the product image size
  2. Config for fotorama product gallery in product details page (PDP)
  3. Determine the breakpoint for mobile view
